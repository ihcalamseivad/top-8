BASEBALL

Popularity: 500 million

Babe Ruth (click image for wiki page)

Baseball is a quintessential bat-and-ball sport that captivates millions with its unique blend of strategy, skill, and tradition. Played between two teams of nine players each, the game unfolds on a diamond-shaped field where the objective is to score runs by hitting a pitched ball and running through a series of four bases arranged in a square. The sport is renowned for its rich history, particularly in the United States, where it is celebrated as the national pastime and has deeply influenced American culture and society. Beyond the U.S., baseball has a fervent following in countries such as Japan, where the Nippon Professional Baseball league is immensely popular, as well as in various Latin American nations like the Dominican Republic, Venezuela, and Cuba, which consistently produce some of the sport's top talent.
